Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password you provide during registration
  • Financial Data: Expense entries including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you input
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the application
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and access times

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the expense tracking service
  • To personalize your experience and display your financial data
  • To improve our application based on user feedback and usage patterns
  • To communicate with you about service updates and security notices
  • To protect against unauthorized access and misuse

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with the following protections:

  • Encryption of sensitive data both in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view stored data
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Hosting Providers: Cloud services that store application data
  • Analytics: Tools that help us understand application usage
  • Email Services: Providers that send transactional emails

These third parties are obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Export: Export your expense data in a portable format

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the support channel provided in the application.
